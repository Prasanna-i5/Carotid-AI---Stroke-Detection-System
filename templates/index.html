How It Works

Image Upload

Upload a carotid artery ultrasound image. The system accepts standard medical imaging formats and preprocesses them automatically.

02

Feature Extraction

GLCM (Gray-Level Co-occurrence Matrix) extracts texture features — contrast, correlation, energy, homogeneity — from the image for SVM analysis.

03
🧠

Dual-Model Analysis

SVM classifies using GLCM texture features. VGG19-CNN analyzes raw pixel data. Both models independently generate predictions and confidence scores.

Medical Context

Carotid Artery Disease & Stroke Risk

Carotid artery stenosis — narrowing of the arteries supplying blood to the brain — is a leading cause of ischemic stroke. Early detection through ultrasound imaging is critical for timely intervention.

Our system analyzes ultrasound images to identify plaque buildup, arterial wall thickness, and texture irregularities that indicate elevated stroke risk before clinical symptoms appear.
